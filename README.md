📊 Coke Sales Analysis using Excel
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Coca-Cola sales data across multiple U.S. retailers to extract insights on sales performance, profitability, and regional trends.

The goal is to answer:

How sales vary by region, retailer, and product
The relationship between pricing, volume, and profitability
Trends in sales and operating margins over time
📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional-level sales data with the following fields:

Retailer and Retailer ID
Region, State, and City
Beverage Brand
Price per Unit
Units Sold
Total Sales
Operating Profit and Operating Margin
Invoice Date and Month
🧹 Data Preparation
Cleaned and standardized column names
Ensured consistent formatting for dates and numeric fields
Validated categorical data (regions, products)
Structured key metrics for analysis
📊 Key Analysis Performed
1. Sales Analysis
Total Sales: $8.68 Million
West region generated the highest revenue
Sales are concentrated among a few key retailers
2. Product Performance
Coca-Cola is the top-performing product
Followed by Dasani Water and Diet Coke
3. Retailer Insights
Sodapop contributes the highest share of revenue
Strong dependency on top retailers observed
4. Time-Based Trends
Peak sales observed in July and December
Indicates seasonal demand patterns
5. Profitability Analysis
Higher sales does not always result in higher profitability
Operating margins vary across products and regions
